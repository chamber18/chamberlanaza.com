HOW WE STARTED

Bahay Kubo Beach Resort, nestled on the sun-kissed shores of Dahican Beach, beckons you to a world of tranquility and cultural immersion. Our name, "Bahay Kubo," translates to "Nipa Hut" in Filipino, reflecting our commitment to embracing the local aesthetic and offering an authentic Filipino experience.

Imagine stepping onto our pristine beach, the soft sand warming your toes as the crystal-clear waves lap at the shore. Breathe in the fresh ocean air, invigorated by the rhythmic sounds of nature. Here, at Bahay Kubo, we invite you to:

  • Unwind in comfort: Choose from a variety of charming accommodations, each designed to provide a haven of relaxation. From traditional nipa huts to modern rooms, we cater to your preferences and budget.
  • Embrace the adventure: Dive into the world-class waves of Dahican, renowned for their perfect breaks. Whether you're a seasoned surfer or a curious beginner, we offer lessons and rentals to help you experience the thrill of riding the waves.
  • Connect with the culture: Immerse yourself in the warmth of Filipino hospitality. Learn about local traditions, crafts, and cuisine, creating lasting memories that go beyond the beach.
  • Reconnect with loved ones: Create unforgettable moments with your family and friends. Build sandcastles, share laughter around a bonfire, or simply relax and soak up the sun together.

Bahay Kubo Beach Resort is more than just a place to stay; it's a gateway to an experience that nourishes your soul. We are dedicated to providing you with exceptional service, genuine connections, and a taste of the real Philippines.

Come, discover your paradise at Bahay Kubo Beach Resort.

2010

The Ramirez family establishes Bahay Kubo Beach Resort, starting with a single, symbolic nipa hut. Their vision: to showcase Filipino hospitality and celebrate the beauty of Dahican Beach.

2011-2013

The resort expands, offering additional nipa hut accommodations to cater to the growing demand from travelers seeking an authentic beach experience.

2014-2016

Bahay Kubo introduces new amenities, including a swimming pool, a restaurant serving delicious Filipino cuisine, and beach activity rentals.

2017-2019

The resort gains recognition for its commitment to sustainability and cultural preservation, attracting eco-conscious travelers and those seeking a deeper connection with Filipino culture.

2020

Bahay Kubo adapts to the challenges of the global pandemic, implementing safety protocols and offering flexible booking options to ensure guests' well-being and peace of mind.

2021-Present

Bahay Kubo continues to thrive, welcoming guests from all over the world and creating lasting memories through exceptional service, cultural experiences, and breathtaking beachfront stays. The resort remains dedicated to its core values of sustainability, cultural immersion, and providing a haven for relaxation and connection.

Bahay Kubo Beach Resort: Mission Statement

Bahay Kubo Beach Resort is dedicated to providing unforgettable experiences that embrace Filipino hospitality, celebrate the natural beauty of Dahican Beach, and foster meaningful connections for our guests. We strive to:

  • Offer a haven of relaxation and adventure through comfortable accommodations, diverse activities, and breathtaking beachfront access.
  • Showcase the richness of Filipino culture by integrating local traditions, cuisine, and crafts into the guest experience.
  • Operate responsibly by implementing sustainable practices and supporting the local community.
  • Exceed expectations through exceptional service, genuine warmth, and a commitment to creating lasting memories for all.
